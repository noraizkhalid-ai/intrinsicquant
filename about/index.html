Our Story

We saw quantitative researchers struggling with fragmented, biased, and noisy data. So we cleaned it up.

IntrinsicQuant was founded in 2025 with a straightforward mission: make institutional-grade financial datasets and quantitative tools accessible beyond the walls of billion-dollar funds.

Legacy data providers routinely fail to adjust for survivorship bias or corporate restructurings, leading to catastrophic backtest errors. The highest quality fundamental data remained locked behind $25k-a-year terminals, leaving independent quants and boutique funds at a structural disadvantage.

We built IntrinsicQuant as a modern data platform that delivers point-in-time fundamentals, on-demand DCF models with adjustable assumptions, market regime indicators, and options flow analytics, all through clean APIs and an intuitive web terminal. Our dedicated North America equities database is our deepest coverage universe, with 50,000+ global equities also supported.

Our team consists of former quantitative researchers, data engineers, and market practitioners who understand that in systematic trading, your edge is only as good as your data.
